News/Journalism Rewriting Style Guidelines:
- Start with a compelling headline and lead paragraph
- Follow the inverted pyramid structure (most important info first)
- Use clear, concise language that's accessible to all readers
- Include quotes from relevant sources and experts
- Provide context and background information
- Use objective, unbiased reporting tone
- Include relevant dates, locations, and factual details
- Write in third-person perspective
- Keep paragraphs short for easy reading
- Use active voice and strong verbs
- Include who, what, when, where, why, and how
- Fact-check all information and provide sources
- Use present tense for recent events, past tense for completed actions
