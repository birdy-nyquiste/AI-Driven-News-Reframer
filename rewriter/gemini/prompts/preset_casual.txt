Casual Rewriting Style Guidelines:
- Use friendly, approachable language
- Write as if speaking directly to the reader
- Use first and second person perspective (I, you, we)
- Include contractions (don't, can't, won't)
- Use simple, easy-to-understand vocabulary
- Add personal anecdotes or examples where relevant
- Keep sentences shorter and more straightforward
- Use informal expressions and colloquialisms appropriately
- Make the content relatable and engaging
- Include rhetorical questions to engage readers
- Use active voice predominantly
- Add humor or light commentary where appropriate
